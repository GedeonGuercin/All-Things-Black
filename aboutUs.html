Princeton Caribbean Connection (PCC)

PCC aims to provide all Princeton students a means for celebrating and learning about the Caribbean.

Princeton Black Men's Association (PBMA)

PBMA promotes and enhances the Black male experience on campus through a range of academic, social, and service events.

Princeton Ethiopian and Eritrean Students Association (PEESA)

Dedicated to bringing some of Ethiopia and Eritrea to Princeton through various cultural, educational, as well as service-oriented events.

DoroBucci

Princeton's premier African dance group which seeks to celebrate and understand African culture through the performing arts.

Princeton African Students Association (PASA)

PASA raises awareness on Africa & Africa-related issues and explores/celebrates the diversity of African cultures to foster unity/friendship amongst members.

Princeton Association of Black Women (PABW)

PABW celebrates, unifies, and educates the community of black women on the Princeton campus.

National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE)

NSBE promotes academic excellence through the form of tutoring, mentoring and incentive programs.
